Private Pay

Private pay allows families to access services directly, without requiring a referral or being limited by insurance visit caps.

Sessions may take place in your home, at a private school, or virtually, depending on what best supports your child.

Virtual Sessions

Virtual sessions are available to families anywhere within the state of California.

Teletherapy is generally not recommended for children under age 5, or for children with attention challenges, lisps, or significantly reduced speech intelligibility, as progress in these areas may be limited without in-person contact.

Strong candidates for virtual speech therapy include clients working on /r/ production, school-age language, fluency, voice, and social pragmatic skills.
