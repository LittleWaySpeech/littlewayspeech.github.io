Sessions may take place in your home, at a private school, or virtually depending on what best supports your child.

Virtual Sessions

Virtual sessions are available to families anywhere within the state of California.

Online speech therapy is not recommended for children under age 5, as well as children with attention deficits, lisps or /s/ production, or significantly low speech intelligibility. Progress may be limited in these areas without in-person sessions.

Good candidates for virtual speech therapy include clients working on /r/ production, school-aged language, fluency, voice, and social pragmatic skills.
