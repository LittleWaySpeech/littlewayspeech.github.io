Rates

In addition to contracting with the San Diego Regional Center and local school districts, Little Way Speech Therapy offers private pay services for families seeking flexible, direct access to care.

Private Pay Information

Private pay means clients are responsible for full payment of services. No doctor’s referral is needed, and there is no limit to the number of visits.

Private pay clients can receive services in the home, at a private school, or virtually.

Rates

  • 60-minute initial evaluation (home visit or virtual): $190
  • 30-minute therapy session (home visit): $65
  • 30-minute therapy session (virtual): $60
  • 50-minute therapy session (home visit): $90
  • 50-minute therapy session (virtual): $85

Travel Fee

Note: For longer travel distances or consistently poor traffic conditions, a $30 travel fee will be added to each service.

Locations to which this fee may apply include, but are not limited to, National City, Chula Vista, Del Mar, Escondido, Lakeside, and Alpine.

Advance notice will be given if this fee applies.

Insurance & Funding Information

Superbills can be provided upon request. A superbill is a detailed invoice that clients can use to request reimbursement from their insurance providers for services already paid.

Your child may also be eligible for services funded by the San Diego Regional Center. Click here to apply.

Virtual sessions can be provided to any client and family located in the State of California.
