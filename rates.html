Rates

Little Way Speech Therapy offers private pay services for families seeking flexible, personalized care, in addition to contracted services through the San Diego Regional Center and local school districts.

Private Pay Information

Private pay allows families to access services directly, without needing a doctor’s referral or being limited by insurance visit caps.

Sessions can take place in your home, at a private school, or virtually—depending on what works best for your family.

Rates

  • 60-minute initial evaluation (home or virtual): $190
  • 30-minute therapy session (home visit): $65
  • 30-minute therapy session (virtual): $60
  • 50-minute therapy session (home visit): $90
  • 50-minute therapy session (virtual): $85

Travel Fee

Important: For longer travel distances or consistently heavy traffic areas, a $30 travel fee may be added per session.

This may apply to locations such as National City, Chula Vista, Del Mar, Escondido, Lakeside, and Alpine.

You will always be informed in advance if this fee applies.

Insurance & Funding

While insurance is not directly accepted, superbills are available upon request. These detailed invoices can be submitted to your insurance provider for possible reimbursement.

Your child may also qualify for services funded through the San Diego Regional Center.

Virtual Sessions

Virtual sessions are not recommended for children under age 5 or children with attention deficits, lisps, low speech inteligibility. Good candidates for virtual therapy include clients working on 'R' production, school-aged language, fluency, voice, and social pragmatic skills.
