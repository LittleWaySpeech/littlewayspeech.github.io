About

At Little Way Speech Therapy, we believe that no act of care is too small when done with great love. Our approach, inspired by the “Little Way” of St. Thérèse of Lisieux, emphasizes patience, compassion, and the belief that small steps can lead to meaningful growth. Even the smallest moments of connection and encouragement can make a lasting difference!
