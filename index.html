Welcome

Little Way Speech Therapy offers individualized, relationship-centered speech and language services for children and families throughout San Diego. Each therapy plan is thoughtfully designed to support meaningful communication, confidence, and connection.

Services

Early Intervention

Play-based, family-centered therapy for infants and toddlers to support early communication, language development, and connection.

School-Age Speech & Language

Individualized therapy for children needing support with articulation, language, social communication, and academic success.

Evaluations

Comprehensive speech and language evaluations to better understand strengths, needs, and the most appropriate next steps.
