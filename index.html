Welcome

Little Way Speech Therapy provides pediatric speech-language services in San Diego County, with a focus on Early Start home health services through the San Diego Regional Center and Independent Educational Evaluations (IEEs).

Each child is approached with patience, compassion, and individualized care, with the belief that small moments of connection can lead to meaningful growth.
