Welcome

Little Way Speech Therapy provides compassionate, individualized speech and language services for children throughout San Diego County. Services are offered in-home and virtually, making support accessible, flexible, and centered around your child’s daily life.

How We Support Your Child

Early Intervention

Play-based, family-centered therapy for infants and toddlers to support early communication, connection, and development.

Speech & Language Therapy

Individualized therapy for children needing support with articulation, language, social communication, and school success.

Comprehensive Evaluations

In-depth assessments to understand your child’s strengths and needs, guiding clear and meaningful next steps.
