Services

We offer individualized, family-centered speech and language services designed to support each child’s unique communication needs. Sessions are engaging, play-based, and tailored to promote meaningful progress.

Areas of Support

Speech Sound Disorders

Support for articulation and phonological development to improve speech clarity and intelligibility.

Language Development

Building both understanding and expressive language skills for more effective communication.

Social Communication

Helping children develop conversational skills, perspective-taking, and meaningful social interaction.

AAC Support

Guidance with augmentative and alternative communication systems, including device selection, funding, and programming.

Early Intervention

Play-based therapy for infants and toddlers to support early communication and developmental milestones.

Parent Coaching

Equipping caregivers with practical, easy-to-use strategies to support communication in everyday routines.

What to Expect

Services typically begin with an initial evaluation, followed by individualized therapy sessions. Close collaboration with families is prioritized to ensure strategies carry over into daily life, supporting consistent and lasting progress.
