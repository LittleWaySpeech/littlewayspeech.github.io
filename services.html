Helping Your Child Thrive

Supporting children with a wide range of communication needs.

Areas of Support

Speech Sound Disorders

Targeting articulation and phonological patterns to improve clarity and overall speech intelligibility.

Language Development

Supporting both receptive and expressive language to strengthen comprehension, grammar, vocabulary, and more.

Social Communication

Building skills for interaction, conversation, and connection, including perspective-taking and pragmatic language.

AAC Support

Providing guidance with Augmentative and Alternative Communication, including device trials, programming, and implementation across environments.

Early Intervention

Play-based therapy for infants and toddlers focused on early communication, interaction, and caregiver support.

Parent Coaching

Practical, supportive strategies that help caregivers foster communication growth within daily routines and natural interactions.

What to Expect

Services typically begin with a comprehensive evaluation to better understand your child’s strengths and areas of need. From there, therapy sessions are individualized and designed to build skills in a supportive, engaging environment.

Collaboration with caregivers is prioritized throughout the process so that strategies can be naturally integrated into everyday routines, supporting meaningful progress beyond each session.

Service Delivery

Services are provided in the home or other natural environments to support comfort, familiarity, and real-life application of skills. Sessions are engaging, play-based, and thoughtfully designed to support meaningful, lasting progress in everyday communication.

Little Way Speech Therapy frequently partners with the San Diego Regional Center. Private pay services are also available for families seeking direct support with flexible scheduling.
