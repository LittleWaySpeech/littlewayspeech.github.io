Early Start Services

After you are approved for speech therapy services through the San Diego Regional Center, you may request Little Way Speech Therapy as your vendor.

Services are limited to select zip codes in San Diego County. Please use the contact form to confirm whether your home is within the service area.

What is an Independent Educational Evaluation (IEE)?

An Independent Educational Evaluation (IEE) is a second opinion regarding your child’s need for special education services in school.

It is a comprehensive evaluation requested by a parent following an IEP evaluation, either because services were denied or because the approved services are felt to be insufficient.

Under federal law, parents are entitled to request an IEE for their child.

You may request that your child’s IEE be completed by Little Way Speech Therapy, even if the practice is not listed as a preferred provider by your school district.
