Early Start

After you are approved for speech therapy services through the San Diego Regional Center, you may request Little Way Speech Therapy as your vendor. Services are limited to select zip codes in San Diego County, so please use the contact form to find out if your home is in range.

What is an IEE?

An Independent Educational Evaluation is a second opinion regarding your child’s need for special education services in school. It is a comprehensive evaluation done at a parent's request following an IEP evaluation, either because a child is denied for special education services or because the services approved are judged by the parent to be inadequate. Under federal law, parents are entitled to an IEE for their child.

You may request that your child’s IEE be completed by Little Way Speech Therapy, even if the practice is not listed as a preferred provider by your school district.
