Personalized Speech Therapy

Supporting children and families across San Diego with compassionate, individualized care.

Little Way Speech Therapy is a pediatric private practice rooted in connection, trust, and meaningful progress. Each child’s path is unique, and therapy is designed to meet them right where they are.

Meet Kat

Kathleen “Kat” Winger, M.S., CCC-SLP, is a licensed pediatric speech-language pathologist serving families since 2019.

Her approach blends evidence-based practice with a warm, relationship-centered style that prioritizes connection and meaningful progress.

Training includes PROMPT, Kaufman, Lidcombe, TalkTools, Orton-Gillingham, Lively Letters, and Natural Language Acquisition.

The practice is owned and operated by Kathleen “Kat” Winger, M.S., CCC-SLP, a licensed pediatric speech-language pathologist serving families since 2019.

Little Way Speech Therapy is grounded in the belief that no act of care is too small when done with great love. Inspired by St. Thérèse of Lisieux’s “Little Way,” this philosophy reflects patience, compassion, and the understanding that small moments of connection lead to meaningful growth.
