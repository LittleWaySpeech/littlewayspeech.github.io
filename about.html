About

Little Way Speech Therapy is a sole proprietorship in San Diego County owned and operated by Kathleen "Kat" Winger, M.S., CCC-SLP, a licensed pediatric speech-language pathologist.

Kat has been working with children of all ages in pediatric private practice since 2019, both in clinic and home health settings. She is trained in PROMPT, Kaufman, Lidcombe, TalkTools, Orton-Gillingham, Lively Letters, and Natural Language Acquisition approaches.

Mission

Little Way Speech is grounded in the belief that no act of care is too small when done with great love. Inspired by St. Thérèse of Lisieux’s “Little Way,” this philosophy reflects patience, compassion, and the belief that small moments of connection can lead to meaningful growth.

Core Values

Individualized Care

Every child’s communication journey is unique, so therapy is tailored to meet each child's needs.

Family Collaboration

A team-based approach supports the best therapy outcomes, so caregiver education is always a priority.

Evidence-Based Practice

Therapy approaches are grounded in research and clinical best practices.
