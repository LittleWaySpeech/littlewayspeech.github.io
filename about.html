Meet Kat

Kathleen “Kat” Winger, M.S., CCC-SLP, is a licensed pediatric speech-language pathologist serving families since 2019.

Her approach blends evidence-based practice with a warm, relationship-centered style that prioritizes connection and meaningful progress.

Kat is trained in PROMPT, Kaufman, Lidcombe, TalkTools, Orton-Gillingham, Lively Letters, and Natural Language Acquisition approaches.

Mission

Little Way Speech Therapy is grounded in the belief that no act of care is too small when done with great love. Inspired by St. Thérèse of Lisieux’s “Little Way,” this philosophy reflects patience, compassion, and the understanding that small moments of connection lead to meaningful growth.
