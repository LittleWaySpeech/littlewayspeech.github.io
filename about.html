Meet Kat

Kathleen "Kat" Winger, M.S., CCC-SLP, is a licensed pediatric speech-language pathologist who has been serving children and families since 2019.

Her approach blends evidence-based practice with a warm, relationship-centered style that prioritizes genuine connection and meaningful progress.

Kat holds specialized training in the Kaufman Speech to Language Protocol (K-SLP), Lidcombe Program, Lively Letters, Meaningful Speech AAC + GLP, Orton-Gillingham informed instruction, PROMPT, Simon Says, and Talk Tools Level 2.

She is also an Ally of Stuttering through Spero Stuttering, reflecting her commitment to ongoing professional development and to listening to and amplifying the voices of people who stutter.

Mission

Little Way Speech Therapy is grounded in the belief that no act of care is too small when done with great love.

Inspired by St. Thérèse of Lisieux's "Little Way," this philosophy reflects patience, compassion, and the understanding that small moments of connection lead to lasting growth.
