About

Little Way Speech Therapy is a pediatric private practice serving children and families throughout San Diego County. Founded and operated by Kathleen “Kat” Winger, M.S., CCC-SLP, the practice is rooted in compassionate, individualized care and a deep commitment to helping children grow in confidence and connection.

Kat is a licensed pediatric speech-language pathologist who has provided services to children of all ages since 2019.

She is trained in PROMPT, Kaufman, Lidcombe, TalkTools, Orton-Gillingham, Lively Letters, and Natural Language Acquisition-informed approaches.

Mission

Little Way Speech Therapy is grounded in the belief that no act of care is too small when done with great love. Inspired by St. Thérèse of Lisieux’s “Little Way,” this philosophy reflects patience, compassion, and the understanding that small moments of connection can lead to meaningful growth.

Core Values

Individualized Care

Every child’s communication journey is unique, so therapy is thoughtfully tailored to support individual strengths, needs, and goals.

Family Collaboration

Meaningful progress happens through partnership, with caregiver support and collaboration at the center of the therapeutic process.

Evidence-Based Practice

Therapy is guided by current research, clinical expertise, and approaches that are responsive to each child’s development.

Let’s Connect

Questions about services, availability, or whether support may be the right fit are always welcome.
