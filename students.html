Graduate Student Clinicians

Little Way Speech Therapy is proud to support the next generation of speech-language pathologists by serving as a clinical education site for graduate student clinicians.

in San Marcos, California

Student clinicians are carefully interviewed and selected before participating in services. They may assist with Early Start home visits under close supervision.

To better support the diverse cultural and linguistic backgrounds of families, bilingual student clinicians are prioritized when available.

Your Child Comes First

Your Choice

Parents and caregivers always have the option to limit or decline student involvement at any time.

Close Supervision

All student participation is guided and supervised to ensure high-quality care and continuity.

Service Scope

Student clinicians are not involved in Independent Educational Evaluations (IEEs).

Questions or Preferences?

You are always welcome to share your preferences or ask questions about student clinician involvement.
