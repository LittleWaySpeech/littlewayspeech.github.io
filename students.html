Supporting the Next Generation of Clinicians

Little Way Speech Therapy is proud to support the next generation of speech-language pathologists.

Student clinicians participate in therapy under close supervision, contributing to a collaborative and supportive learning environment while maintaining the highest standards of care.

Educational Partnership

San Marcos, California

Student clinicians are carefully interviewed and selected before participating in services. They may assist with Early Start home visits under close supervision.

To better support the diverse cultural and linguistic backgrounds of families, bilingual student clinicians are prioritized when available.

Close Supervision

All student participation is guided and supervised to ensure high-quality care and continuity.

Service Scope

Student clinicians are not involved in Independent Educational Evaluations (IEEs).
