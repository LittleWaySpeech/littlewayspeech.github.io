Graduate Student Clinicians

Little Way Speech Therapy is proud to support the next generation of speech-language pathologists by serving as a clinical education site.

Student clinicians participate in therapy under close supervision, contributing to a collaborative and supportive learning environment while maintaining the highest standards of care.

Student Involvement

Graduate student clinicians are carefully selected through an interview process before participating in services. When involved, they may assist during sessions as part of their clinical training experience.

To better support the diverse cultural and linguistic backgrounds of families, bilingual student clinicians are prioritized whenever possible.

Your Child Comes First

Your Choice

Parent and caregiver preferences are always respected. You may choose to limit or decline student involvement at any time.

Close Supervision

All student participation is directly supervised to ensure consistency, quality, and continuity of care.

Scope of Services

Student clinicians do not participate in Independent Educational Evaluations (IEEs).

Questions or Preferences?

You are always welcome to share your preferences or ask questions about student clinician involvement.
