Supporting the Next Generation of Clinicians

Little Way Speech Therapy proudly serves as a clinical training site for graduate student clinicians.

Student clinicians participate in therapy under close supervision, contributing to a collaborative and supportive learning environment while maintaining the highest standards of care.

What This Means for Your Child

Graduate student clinicians are carefully selected through an interview process and may assist during sessions as part of their clinical training.

When available, bilingual student clinicians are prioritized to better support the diverse cultural and linguistic backgrounds of families.
