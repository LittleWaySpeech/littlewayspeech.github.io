Student Interns

Little Way Speech Therapy partners with the University of St. Augustine for Health Sciences (USAHS) in San Marcos, California, as a clinical education site for graduate student clinicians.

Graduate student clinicians are carefully interviewed and selected, and they often assist with Early Start home visits. To better support the diverse cultural and linguistic backgrounds of the families served, bilingual student clinicians are given priority.

Parents and caregivers maintain the right to limit or decline student involvement. Student clinicians are not involved with Independent Educational Evaluations (IEEs).
