Student Interns

Supporting the next generation of speech-language pathologists while maintaining high-quality care for families.

Little Way Speech Therapy partners with the University of St. Augustine for Health Sciences (USAHS) in San Marcos, California, as a clinical education site for graduate student clinicians.

Graduate student clinicians are carefully interviewed and selected and may assist with Early Start home visits. To better support the diverse cultural and linguistic backgrounds of the families served, bilingual student clinicians are given priority.

Parents and caregivers always maintain the right to limit or decline student involvement. Student clinicians are not involved with Independent Educational Evaluations (IEEs).
